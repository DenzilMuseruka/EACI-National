We Are EACI

Evangelical Assemblies of Christ International (EACI) is a Christ-centered, Bible-believing fellowship committed to the proclamation of the Gospel of Jesus Christ and the advancement of God’s Kingdom across nations. Rooted in the authority of Scripture and empowered by the Holy Spirit, we exist to evangelize the lost, disciple believers, and raise servant leaders who live out their faith with purpose, obedience, and love for God.

Our Purpose.

Our purpose is to glorify God by faithfully proclaiming the Gospel of Jesus Christ and building a strong, Spirit-filled Church grounded in biblical truth. We equip believers to grow in faith, maturity, and godly character, enabling them to impact their families, communities, and nations for Christ.

Through worship, discipleship, missions, and compassionate outreach, EACI strengthens local assemblies and advances the work of God wherever He sends us.

Our Mission.

Our mission is to fulfill the Great Commission by preaching the Gospel, making disciples, and equipping believers for effective Christian service. We raise leaders grounded in the Word of God, led by the Holy Spirit, and committed to serving with humility, integrity, and love.

As an international movement, we work across cultures and nations to establish, support, and strengthen churches for the expansion of God’s Kingdom.

For God so loved the world, that he gave his only Son, that whoever believes in him should not perish but have eternal life. For God did not send his Son into the world to condemn the world, but in order that the world might be saved through him.

John 3:16–17 (ESV)

Meet Our Leadership.

Apostle P. Makombe General Overseer

Apostle P. Makombe serves as the General Overseer of Evangelical Assemblies of Christ International, providing spiritual oversight and apostolic leadership across all EACI assemblies. His ministry focuses on sound biblical teaching, discipleship, leadership development, and the advancement of God’s Kingdom through evangelism and church growth.

Pastor Tanya Radzwajohn Pastor

Pastor Tanya Radzwajohn serves within EACI with a heart for pastoral care, discipleship, and ministry support. She is committed to nurturing spiritual growth and strengthening believers through teaching, prayer, and service.

Pastor Moses Nyahada Teaching Pastor
