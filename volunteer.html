Serve With Us

Discover how your unique gifts can make an eternal impact

Here's the truth: God created you with specific talents, passions, and abilities for a reason! You're not just a seat-filler at church — you're a vital part of what God is doing. Whether you thrive behind the scenes or love being on the front lines, there's a perfect place for you to serve and grow.

Other Ways to Serve

Beyond our core ministries, there are many practical ways to serve and make a difference every week!

Ushering & Hospitality

Be the friendly face that welcomes people as they arrive! Help guests find seats, answer questions, and ensure everyone feels at home from the moment they walk through our doors.

Prayer Team

Stand in the gap through intercessory prayer! Join our prayer warriors who meet regularly to pray for our church, community, and nation.

Administrative Support

Use your organizational skills to help with office tasks, event planning, communication, and other behind-the-scenes work that keeps our church running smoothly.

Media & Technology

Help operate sound, lighting, video recording, or live streaming to ensure our services reach people both in-person and online.

Facilities & Maintenance

Keep our church buildings and grounds clean, safe, and welcoming. Whether it's cleaning, repairs, gardening, or setup for events, every task matters.

Community Outreach

Serve our local community through compassion projects, food distribution, visiting the sick, or other practical ways to show God's love in action.
