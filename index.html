Welcome to EACI

Evangelical Assemblies of Christ International (EACI) is a Christ-centered evangelical and Pentecostal fellowship committed to proclaiming the gospel, making disciples, and planting assemblies across nations. Our mission is to unite believers, strengthen communities, and empower individuals to live in faith, service, and devotion to Christ.

Serve With Us.

At Evangelical Assemblies of Christ International, we believe that every believer is called to serve. Through our local assemblies and global initiatives, volunteers play a vital role in advancing the work of the Gospel, strengthening communities, and supporting ministry across nations. There is a place for you to serve and make an eternal impact.

Get Involved

Join a Connect Group.

Connect Groups are the heart of fellowship at EACI. They provide a place for believers to grow in faith, study the Word, pray together, and build meaningful relationships beyond the main worship services. Join a Connect Group near you and experience life together in Christ.
