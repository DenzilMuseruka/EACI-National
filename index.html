Upcoming Events

What's Happening

From family camps to youth gatherings — events where faith comes alive.

Global Children & Families Camp

A time of spiritual growth, teaching, worship, and fellowship for children and families across our international assemblies. Designed to nurture faith and godly values in the next generation.

  • Dates announced by regional assemblies
  • Full-day sessions
  • Hosted by local EACI assemblies worldwide

International Prayer Gathering

United prayer across nations as we seek God’s guidance, renewal, and empowerment for the Church, families, and communities.

  • Scheduled by region
  • Evening sessions
  • On-site and online

Youth Convergence

A dynamic gathering for young people focused on identity in Christ, leadership development, worship, and impact-driven faith.

  • Annual regional gatherings
  • Weekend sessions
  • Regional EACI centres

Leadership & Ministers Conference

Apostolic teaching and strategic equipping for pastors, leaders, and ministers serving within Evangelical Assemblies of Christ International.

  • Dates communicated by EACI leadership
  • Multiple teaching sessions
  • International & regional venues

New Here?

What to Expect

We know trying a new church can feel uncertain. Here's what to expect so you can relax and be yourself.

1

Come As You Are

Jeans and a t-shirt? Perfect. Dress clothes? Also great. We care more about your heart than your outfit.

2

90-Minute Service

Uplifting worship, a practical message, and friendly people. Arrive 10 minutes early to grab a seat. Kids programs run at the same time.

3

Warm Welcome

Our greeters will help you find a seat, show you around, and answer any questions. No pressure — just a warm welcome.
